SQL Analysis

The raw queries used to extract insights from the database.

Aggregation

1. Calculate Total Company Revenue

SELECT ROUND(SUM(total), 2) AS total_revenue
FROM invoices;
Grouping

2. Revenue Generated by Country

SELECT billing_country, ROUND(SUM(total), 2) AS revenue
FROM invoices
GROUP BY billing_country
ORDER BY revenue DESC;
Joins

3. Top 10 Customers by Spending

SELECT c.first_name || ' ' || c.last_name AS customer_name, 
       ROUND(SUM(i.total), 2) AS total_spent
FROM customers c
JOIN invoices i ON c.customer_id = i.customer_id
GROUP BY customer_name
ORDER BY total_spent DESC
LIMIT 10;
Multi-Join

4. Most Popular Music Genres

SELECT g.name AS genre, COUNT(ii.invoice_line_id) AS tracks_sold
FROM invoice_items ii
JOIN tracks t ON ii.track_id = t.track_id
JOIN genres g ON t.genre_id = g.genre_id
GROUP BY genre
ORDER BY tracks_sold DESC;
