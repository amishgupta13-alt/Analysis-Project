ðŸ’¡ SQL Queries & Business Questions

1. What is the total revenue of the company? 
SELECT 
    ROUND(SUM(total), 2) AS total_revenue
FROM invoices;
2. Which countries generate the most revenue? 
SELECT 
    billing_country,
    ROUND(SUM(total), 2) AS revenue
FROM invoices
GROUP BY billing_country
ORDER BY revenue DESC;
3. Who are the top 10 customers by spending? 
SELECT 
    c.first_name || ' ' || c.last_name AS customer_name,
    ROUND(SUM(i.total), 2) AS total_spent
FROM customers c
JOIN invoices i ON c.customer_id = i.customer_id
GROUP BY customer_name
ORDER BY total_spent DESC
LIMIT 10;
4. Which music genres are most popular? 
SELECT 
    g.name AS genre,
    COUNT(ii.invoice_line_id) AS tracks_sold
FROM invoice_items ii
JOIN tracks t ON ii.track_id = t.track_id
JOIN genres g ON t.genre_id = g.genre_id
GROUP BY genre
ORDER BY tracks_sold DESC;
